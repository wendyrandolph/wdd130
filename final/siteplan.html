Widow Life 101

Wendy Randolph

Overview

Purpose

To help those who find themselves in the role of being a widow or a widower, and really want to find an easy,
access to resources such as books, movies, talks, quotes and other resources that will help navigate through
the grieving process especially in those early days.

Audience

Any man or woman who finds themselves a widow or widower and has zero idea of what to do, or what might help in their new life.
