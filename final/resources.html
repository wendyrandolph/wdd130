LDS W/W Conferences

Every year hundreds of widows and widowers gather for a conference to attend classes on a wide variety of topics related to the loss of a spouse, grief, finances, even dating, and remarriage and blending families. The classes change from year to year and the content is helpful in whatever stage you might be in. This last year due to covid-19 we held a virtual conference instead. Here are links to YouTube Videos for the audio of:

  • Kent Allen on Grief
  • Dating Do's And Don'ts by Kent Allen
  • Christy Kane - The Importance of Connection
LDS W/W Ministering Angels

If you are a widow or widower in need of financial assistance you can apply for help through this link. You can also donate to this organization to help out those in need who find themselves in an upside down world and sometimes in a precarious situation financially.
